Guiding principles

We're building Dbljump because we want a video game reference with these foundations. We have a lot of work ahead.

  1. Strive for high standards

    Publish content that is accurate, neutral, and transparent about sources.

  2. Be respectful

    Properly credit creators. Welcome everyone, because everyone deserves to enjoy gaming.

  3. Keep evolving

    Keep making Dbljump better by improving existing features and developing new ones.

  4. Honour the medium

    Shine light on game creators and their achievements. Be a positive representative for gaming.

  5. Be useful

    Deliver the best and most focused gaming reference, and give readers the best experience, that we can.
